Help

What it does: Applies a double-stroke style to GPX/SVG tracks directly in your browser.

  1. Upload your .svg file.
  2. Choose outer and inner colors.
  3. Adjust stroke factors.
  4. Set the Group ID (default trk1).
  5. Optionally remove watermark text.
  6. Click Generate styled SVG and download the result.

Color tips: Use names like red, yellow, or hex codes like #ff0000, #ffff00.
